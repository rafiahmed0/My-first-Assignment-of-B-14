Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a student discount available?

Yes! We offer a 20% discount for valid student ID holders. Please email us your ID after booking.

What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the conference start date.

Will the sessions be recorded, and how can I access them?

Yes, all talks and workshops will be recorded. Standard ticket holders get access for 30 days, while Pro and Team ticket holders get lifetime access to the video archive.

Do I need to bring my own laptop for the workshops?

Yes, please bring your own laptop with your preferred code editor installed. We will send out a list of required software/tools dependencies a week before the event.

Are food and refreshments included in the ticket price?

Yes! All ticket tiers include access to the networking lunch, morning/evening coffee breaks, and refreshments throughout the 3-day event.

Can I upgrade my ticket from Standard to Pro later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade your ticket at any time before the event starts by paying the price difference, provided that Pro tickets are still available.
